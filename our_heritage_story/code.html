Our Essence

The Art of
Timekeeping

At Horológe, we believe time is not merely a measurement, but a canvas for artistic expression. Every component is hand-finished, every movement is a symphony of physics and aesthetics.

Our philosophy is rooted in the "Golden Ratio" of Swiss watchmaking—balancing tradition with the avant-garde to create pieces that transcend generations.

"Precision is our minimum standard. Beauty is our ultimate goal."

Our Heritage

1924

Founded in Geneva

Master watchmaker Henri Laurent opens the first Horológe atelier on the banks of Lake Geneva.

1952

Chronometer Patent

Introduction of the revolutionary H-1 movement, setting new global standards for magnetic resistance.

1988

Global Expansion

Horológe opens flagship boutiques in Paris, New York, and Tokyo, becoming a symbol of global luxury.
